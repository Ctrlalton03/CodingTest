Welcome to our idyllic woodland retreat! We're thrilled to share an array of delightful experiences with you during your stay. Savor the enchanting flavors of our region at Forest Haven Dining Retreat, where our culinary team crafts delectable dishes using both local and international ingredients, all set against the backdrop of the serene woods. For those seeking adventure, explore the natural wonders that surround us with guided hikes, bird-watching excursions, and serene kayaking outings. After a day of exploration, unwind and rejuvenate in our Finnish-style sauna, where the soothing warmth and fragrant woodsy ambiance promise relaxation like no other. And for those who prefer to stay active, our modern gym offers state-of-the-art equipment amidst stunning forest views. Our dedicated staff is here to ensure your experience is truly memorable, whether you're indulging in fine dining, immersing in nature, or relaxing in our wellness facilities.

Our Rooms

Our Forest View Deluxe Room offers a tranquil forest escape with its warm, earthy tones and natural textures that seamlessly blend with the woodland surroundings. This inviting space features a plush king-sized bed, a private balcony for immersing yourself in the lush green canopy, and modern amenities such as a flat-screen TV, high-speed Wi-Fi, and a well-stocked minibar. The en-suite bathroom boasts a spacious walk-in shower and eco-friendly toiletries inspired by the forest's natural scents, ensuring your comfort and relaxation. Whether you're seeking a romantic retreat or a nature-inspired adventure, this room provides the perfect haven to enjoy the comforts of modern luxury while immersing yourself in the serenity of the woods.
